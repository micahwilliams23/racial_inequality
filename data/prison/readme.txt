Prisoners in 2018		
		
This zip archive contains tables in individual  .csv spreadsheets		
from Prisoners in 2018, NCJ 253516. The full report including text		
and graphics in pdf format is available from: https://www.bjs.gov/index.cfm?ty=pbdetail&iid=6846		
		
This report is one in a series.  More recent editions may be available.  To view a list of all in the series go to		
https://www.bjs.gov/index.cfm?ty=pbse&sid=40
